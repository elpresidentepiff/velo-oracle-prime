CHRONOLOGICAL CROSS-VALIDATION METHODOLOGY
Data sorted strictly by race date to prevent lookahead bias—models never see future data during training
Training data always comes before test data in chronological order, mimicking real-world deployment
5-fold validation with expanding training window ensures robust performance estimation
Example: Train on 2020-2022 data → Test on Q1 2023 → Expand to train on 2020-Q1 2023 → Test on Q2 2023
